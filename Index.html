About Me

I am a creator. I want to make things that make life easier or more enjoyable. Whether it is programming, games, or art, I am making things that do useful tasks, provide a unique experience for the user, or make people laugh.

I initially began college as a student in engineering at the University of Wisconsin in Platteville. Like coding, engineering is all about creating practical solutions. However, after the financial crisis of 2008, I learned that I had a passion for economics. Eventually I ended up switching majors and schools, earning a degree in economics from Indiana University Purdue University Fort Wayne (IPFW). I am currently learning the .Net Framework to complement my knowledge with practical skills that I can use to build things.
